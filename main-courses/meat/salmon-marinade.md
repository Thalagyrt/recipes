# Salmon Marinade

## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup pineapple juice
- 2 tbs soy sauce
- 2 tbs brown sugar
- 1 tsp bourbon
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1/8 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil

## Instructions

- Combine and pour over two 8 oz. filets and let marinate for 1-3 hours, reserve some mixture to brush over salmon while you grill.
